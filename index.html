ABOUT

Geometry Dash Meltdown is a free ad-supported expansion app of Geometry Dash developed and published by RobTop Games and released on 19 December 2015 for Android and iOS. It features 3 exclusive levels, with limited achievements, icons and collectibles.

TIPS

The game uses visual effects to create an exciting experience that matches its rhythmic gameplay. As you play through levels, you'll see glowing elements that help guide you to the finish line, so it's important to pay attention to these platforms and obstacles. However, some flashy visuals can be distracting, so you need to focus on the important elements to avoid getting stressed and stay on track
